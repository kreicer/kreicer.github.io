About

I’m Dmitry Listopadov, a Staff DevOps Engineer and Cloud Architect.

I build engineering platforms for teams that have outgrown fragmented infrastructure and manual delivery. Over the past 10 years, my work has focused on the intersection of cloud architecture and platform engineering: Kubernetes, GitOps, CI/CD, identity, and observability. I’ve built platforms from scratch, led cloud migrations, and standardized how multiple teams ship software.

Why Kreicer Krew?#

When you spend enough time managing infrastructure, you start noticing the same operational bottlenecks over and over. I created Kreicer Krew as a laboratory to solve these specific pain points.

Instead of writing throwaway bash scripts, I build focused, composable CLI tools in Go. If you work with Kubernetes or ArgoCD, you might find them useful. You can check them out on the Projects page.

Mentorship & Advisory#

I strongly believe in sharing knowledge. I regularly advise teams on infrastructure architecture, platform engineering, and cloud migrations. I also mentor DevOps engineers who want to level up their careers.

If you are looking for an architectural review, need help with your platform design, or want to discuss a mentorship session, feel free to reach out to me directly.
